What it controls

Type what you need in plain English. FlowState routes it to the right macOS app and executes silently.

Calendar & Scheduling

Am I free at 3?

Check your day, create events, find free slots, detect conflicts. Handles "next Tuesday," recurring meetings, and cross-calendar queries.

Music & Podcasts

Play something chill for focusing

Play, pause, skip, search by artist or mood. Queue management, volume control, and podcast episodes — all in the background.

Messages & Mail

Email Sarah about the 3 PM meeting

Send iMessages, check unread email, compose replies. Resolves contacts by name — works across Mail and Messages.

System Controls

DND on and dim the screen

Volume, brightness, dark mode, DND, Focus modes, battery, lock, sleep. Say "it's too bright" — FlowState lowers the brightness.

Files & Finder

Find the budget spreadsheet

Spotlight search, open files, organize folders, check storage. Finds that PDF from last week even if you forgot the name.

Web Browser

Check reviews for that new restaurant

Navigate websites, fill forms, interact with any web app. Uses your existing logged-in browser sessions — no extra login.

Three keystrokes

Option+Space opens FlowState. Type what you need. Press Return. Done.

1

Type a task in natural language

"Schedule a meeting with John tomorrow at 2pm" or "Play Bohemian Rhapsody" or "Turn on Dark Mode" — just describe what you want done.

2

Claude classifies and routes

The task is analyzed instantly. Calendar events go to Mac Automator. Web tasks go to Playwright. Shortcuts go to Apple Shortcuts. The right tool, every time.

3

Executed silently in the background

No windows pop up. No focus stolen. The task completes while you keep working. Check the result when you're ready.
